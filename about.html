About Us

Who We Are

RateMyTower.com was founded in 2003 and is headquartered in beautiful Dubai, UAE. RateMyTower.com is part of the ReLoMi family. RateMyTower.com is the largest online rating community for rental housing, reaching apartment hunters nationwide.

More apartments

We provide information about more buildings than any other online apartment hunting service. Since we don't charge communities to be listed on RateMyTower.com, we can provide information about all communities, not just the ones who advertise.

More ratings and reviews

We're the largest source for tower and renter reviews of building communities.

Unbiased

Unlike other online apartment hunting services (and even some ratings sites), we do not accept advertising from apartment owners and managers. Renters should never have to worry that we've removed information to please an apartment community. We serve renters, period.

Our Mission

Provide transparent and honest information that enables renters and property managers to improve the evolving concept of "home". In concert with our RateMyTower brand, we aim to strengthen relationships throughout the multifamily industry by creating a platform to attract, understand and retain renters based on leading edge technology, data, and education.
