Writing Tips

Help your fellow renters get the most out of your review with the following tips: 

Be Specific

Don't just complain about something; rather, describe specific things you do or don't like. Explain what happened. We've found users lend more weight to a review with details.

Be Courteous and Honest

Describe your experience, be it good or bad, but don't go overboard. Keep your review precise and to the point. We value open expression, but profanity and hateful speech are not allowed on the site.

No Personal Info

We don't allow reviews with names, phone numbers, email or other contact info, so please avoid using it. 

Photos and Video Tips

Help residents visualize what it will be like to live at a community with the following tips:

Quality

Photos and videos posted by the residents living at a community help prospects imagine what their life will look like if they move-in. Capture your environment and share how you’ve made your space your own. What would you have liked to see before you moved in? Refrain uploading blurry or dark imagery.

No Misconduct Tolerated

All photos and videos submitted will go through review process. If your upload contains any nudity, profanity, or personal information, it will not be posted.
